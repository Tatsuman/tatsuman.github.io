Mission Statement

If you manage your food by this app, you will never spoil your food or take too much time to find a recipe and cook. Manage your food expiration date in this app and you can even know what to buy next by looking up the recipe.

Target - 18 - 24, male/female

Goal - 10k

Product Summary

- Food Managing and Recipe Recommendation App -

User can manage their food expiration dates and look up recipe based on what they have in their fridge. Notification for close expiration dates and suggest recipes to not waste the foods. When user lookup recipe, the app will shows up what they need to buy. User can create their original recipe in the app.

Localization will be execute when the app is opened first time to avoid to not show the recipe that need ingredient you cannot get in the country.

Mechanics

Basic Functions
  • Add ingredient or seasoning in database(fridge, cruet stand)
  • Search recipe that use ingredients in fridge
  • Create their original recipe
  • Look up recipe online
Additional Functions
  • Alert for coming up food expiration
  • Put amount of dish washing for each recipe
  • Original recipe by user will be able to see by other user always
  • Get premium account to make recipes private

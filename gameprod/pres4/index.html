Product Summary

Food Management and Recipe Recommendation App

Device: iOS/Android

Target: US, 18 - 80, male/female

User can manage their food expiration dates and look up recipes based on what they have in their fridge. Notify for close expiration dates and suggest recipes to not waste the foods. When user lookup recipe, the app will shows up what they need to buy. User can create their original recipe in the app.

Personas

Blake - 22

Blake is a college student at San Francisco. He has 5 classes in a semester usually. Because of his busy schedule, he sometime forget about food expiration and spoil his food in the fridge.

Dona - 36

Dona is a mother of a 2 years old kid. She had almost never cooked before she got married. So she is terrible for cooking and food management but her husband are always busy for his work so she need to cook for her family.

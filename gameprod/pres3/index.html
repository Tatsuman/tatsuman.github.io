Product Summary

- Food Managing and Recipe Recommendation App -

Device iOS/Android

Target 18 - 24, male/female

Goal 10k users

User can manage their food expiration dates and look up recipes based on what they have in their fridge. Notify for close expiration dates and suggest recipes to not waste the foods. When user lookup recipe, the app will shows up what they need to buy. User can create their original recipe in the app.

Personas

Blake

Blake is a college student at San Francisco. He has 5 classes in a semester usually. Because of his busy schedule, he sometime forget about food expiration and spoil his food in the fridge.

Erika

Erika work at restaurant in San Francisco. She is trying to eat dinner in her house after work but because of variable schedule at restaurant, she sometime don't open her fridge for few days so easily forget about food expiration dates.

Mechanics

Basic Functions
  • Add ingredient or seasoning in database(fridge, cruet stand)
  • Search recipe that use ingredients in fridge
  • Create their original recipe
  • Look up recipe online
Additional Functions
  • Alert for coming up food expiration
  • Put amount of dish washing for each recipe
  • Original recipe by user will be able to see by other user always
  • Get premium account to make recipes private

Recommendation (Top Page)

User can know what they can cook from this screen. The recipes are listed up in order of close expiration date of foods in their fridge.

Managing Foods

User can add foods and manage those expiration dates. For the things that doesn't have specific erxpiration dates, user will be able to type a bought dates instead.

Search Recipes

User will be able to search recipes from database to look up what they want. Suggest what ingredients that they should use to not spoil foods.

Search Results

Show up recipes that they can cook with what they already have.
